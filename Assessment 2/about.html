How does it work?

Psychotherapy enables people suffering from a mental disorder to:

  • Learn how to modify the behaviors, emotions, and ideas that contribute to their illness.
  • Understand and identify the life problems or events that contribute to their illness, such as a major illness, a death in the family, a job loss, or a divorce, and assist them in determining which aspects of those problems they may be able to solve or improve.
  • Regain control and pleasure in your life.
  • Learn healthy coping strategies and problem-solving techniques.

What is exactly is it?

Talk therapy, also known as psychotherapy, is a type of mental health treatment.

It is frequently used to treat mental disorders, alone or in combination with medications. You talk to a doctor or a licensed psychological health care professional during a psychotherapy session to identify and change troubling thoughts.

Our approach on PsychoTherapy

Psychotherapy can be used to treat a variety of mental disorders, including:

  • Depression
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Anxiety
  • Anorexia, bulimia, and other eating disorders
  • Posttraumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
  • Schizophrenia
  • Addictions
  • Personality Disorders

why choose us?

About 75 percent of people who enter psychotherapy show some benefit from it. Psychotherapy has been shown to improve emotions and behaviors and to be linked with positive changes in the brain and body. The benefits also include fewer sick days, less disability, fewer medical problems, and increased work satisfaction.

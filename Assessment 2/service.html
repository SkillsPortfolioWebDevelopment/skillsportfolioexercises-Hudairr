Individual Therapy:

This therapy involves only the patient and the therapist.

Group Therapy:

Two or more patients may participate in therapy at the same time. Patients are able to share experiences and learn that others feel the same way and have had the same experiences.

Marital Therapy:

This type of therapy helps spouses and partners understand why their loved one has a mental disorder, what changes in communication and behaviors can help, and what they can do to cope. This type of therapy can also be used to help a couple that is struggling with aspects of their relationship.

Couples Therapy:

This type of therapy can also be used to help a couple that is struggling with aspects of their relationship.

Career Therapy:

Career counseling is a type of advice-giving and support provided by career counselors to their clients, to help the clients manage their journey through life, learning and work changes.

Family Therapy:

Because family is a key part of the team that helps people with mental illness get better, it is sometimes helpful for family members to understand what their loved one is going through, how they themselves can cope, and what they can do to help.

Life Coaching:

If you are struggling in more than one problem from the ones stated above, A full life coaching session that will span along months will be required.

why choose us?

About 75 percent of people who enter psychotherapy show some benefit from it. Psychotherapy has been shown to improve emotions and behaviors and to be linked with positive changes in the brain and body. The benefits also include fewer sick days, less disability, fewer medical problems, and increased work satisfaction.
